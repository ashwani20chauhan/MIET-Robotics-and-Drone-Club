Robotics Club: Engineering Automation

Established in 2018 to build technical expertise and innovation in robotics.

Mission and Core Objectives

Technical Platform

To provide a technical platform and resources for students interested in robotics development.

Project Development

To prepare robots-based technical projects for different functionalities.

Skill Cultivation

To participate in competitions and cultivate technical plus soft skills.

Innovation Ecosystem

To create an ecosystem for innovation and transfer of information.
