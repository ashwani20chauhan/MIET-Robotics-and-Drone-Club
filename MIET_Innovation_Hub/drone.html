Drone Club: Mastering UAV Technology

Started in July 2022 to provide hands-on experience in building and deploying drones.

Core Focus and Future Skills

Project Development

Provide guidance and resources to prepare various types of drones and drone-based technical projects.

Competition Platform

Provide a platform to participate in drone competitions and cultivate technical plus soft skills.

Drone Pilot License

The club aims to prepare students for the official drone pilot license.
